## What This Folder Is

This is not a style guide. It is the full conviction system behind the brand -- the thesis, the positioning, the audience architecture, and the execution strategy. If you are writing copy, planning a campaign, briefing a visual, or making a channel decision, start here.

The Sales team operates from `/Websites/sales/`. That folder handles prospects, pipeline, and outreach execution. This folder handles everything that makes sales work: brand credibility, audience clarity, and strategic positioning.

## How to Use This Folder

**Writing copy?** Start with [01-BRAND-THESIS.md](./01-BRAND-THESIS.md) to understand what the brand believes, then go to [05-BRAND-VOICE.md](./05-BRAND-VOICE.md) for how it speaks.

**Planning a campaign?** Read the relevant audience profile ([03](./03-AUDIENCE-DTC.md) or [04](./04-AUDIENCE-TRADE.md)), then build the campaign in [07-CAMPAIGNS.md](./07-CAMPAIGNS.md).

**Briefing visuals?** Add a brief to [06-VISUAL-IDENTITY.md](./06-VISUAL-IDENTITY.md). Design picks it up from there.

**Deciding which channel to use?** See [09-CHANNELS.md](./09-CHANNELS.md) for strategy and priority by platform.

**Tracking performance?** Log results in [10-ANALYTICS.md](./10-ANALYTICS.md) after every campaign and monthly.

**Working on B2B / trade?** This folder handles brand positioning for trade. Sales execution (prospects, pipeline, outreach) lives in `/Websites/sales/`.

## Ground Rules

**1. The thesis governs everything.**  
[01-BRAND-THESIS.md](./01-BRAND-THESIS.md) is not background reading -- it is the filter every decision passes through. If a campaign, post, or piece of copy cannot be traced back to the thesis, it does not ship.

**2. Know who you are writing for.**  
Every piece of copy targets a specific segment. Name the audience before writing a word. See [03-AUDIENCE-DTC.md](./03-AUDIENCE-DTC.md) and [04-AUDIENCE-TRADE.md](./04-AUDIENCE-TRADE.md).

**3. No filler. No cliches. No exclamation marks.**  
These are not preferences -- they are rules. See [05-BRAND-VOICE.md](./05-BRAND-VOICE.md) for the full list.

**4. Marketing enables sales. Sales closes.**  
The brand assets in this folder exist to make Floki credible enough to earn the meeting. The Sales team closes the deal. See `/Websites/sales/02-SALES-STRATEGY.md` for B2B channel priorities.

**5. Visual briefs go through the process.**  
Do not assign visual work informally. Add a brief to [06-VISUAL-IDENTITY.md](./06-VISUAL-IDENTITY.md) and let the design workflow run. See `Websites/design/03-AI-DESIGN-WORKFLOW.md`.

**6. Update analytics after every campaign.**  
[10-ANALYTICS.md](./10-ANALYTICS.md) is only useful if it reflects reality. Log results. Run post-mortems. Update [07-CAMPAIGNS.md](./07-CAMPAIGNS.md) when campaigns close.
