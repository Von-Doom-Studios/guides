## Wholesale Marketplace Platforms

Listing on wholesale marketplaces creates passive inbound retail discovery. Retailers browse these platforms looking for new products. Set it up once and let it run.

### 1. Faire (faire.com)

**What it is:** Largest wholesale marketplace for boutique retailers. 700,000+ active retailers globally. Handles Net-60 payment terms and pays Floki immediately on each order.

**Commission:** 15% on new retailer accounts, 0% on returning accounts you bring to Faire  
**Apply:** faire.com/brand-application  
**Setup requirements:** Product catalog, product photos, wholesale pricing, brand story  
**Why it matters:** Once listed, retailers discover Floki passively while browsing the marketplace. No outreach required.

**How to get featured on Faire:**
- Complete 100% of brand profile (logo, bio, product photos, pricing)
- Start with competitive pricing (use floor wholesale price to attract first orders)
- Get 5+ first-time orders within 60 days of launch - this signals the algorithm
- Respond to retailer inquiries within 24 hours (Faire tracks response time)
- Apply for "Faire Select" badge after 10+ orders (unlocks more visibility)
- Use Faire's built-in email tool to invite your existing retailer prospects directly to your Faire store - they can order without you paying commission

### 2. Tundra (tundra.com)

**What it is:** Wholesale marketplace with 0% commission. Floki keeps 100% of wholesale revenue.

**Commission:** 0%  
**Apply:** tundra.com/sell  
**Why list:** No downside - zero commission means even a single order is pure margin. Good for testing new products.

**How to get featured:** Complete brand profile fully, use Tundra's promoted listing feature ($50/month) to appear in top results.

### 3. Abound (abound.com)

**What it is:** Wholesale marketplace focused on emerging and independent brands. Strong in gift, home, and lifestyle categories.

**Commission:** 15% on orders  
**Apply:** abound.com/sell  
**Why list:** Abound actively curates and promotes new brands through editorial features. More likely to get editorial exposure than Faire.

**How to get featured on Abound:**
- Apply for Abound's "Trending Brands" feature (submit via their editorial inquiry form)
- Use Abound's featured listing option for new categories
- Abound sends weekly "new arrivals" emails to 50,000+ buyers - new brands often appear in these

### 4. Bulletin (bulletin.co)

**What it is:** Wholesale marketplace specifically for mission-driven and women-owned or minority-owned brands. Strong editorial focus.

**Commission:** 15% on orders  
**Apply:** bulletin.co/wholesale/apply  
**Why list:** Floki's brand story (African-inspired design, luxury accessible) fits Bulletin's editorial narrative. Higher chance of being featured.

**How to get featured:**
- Highlight brand story (cultural inspiration, product design philosophy) in brand application
- Apply for Bulletin's "Brand Spotlight" feature
- Bulletin's buyer newsletter reaches 40,000+ boutique buyers

### 5. Modalyst (modalyst.com)

**What it is:** Dropship and wholesale marketplace. Good for DTC crossover - retailers list your product for dropship without buying inventory upfront.

**Commission:** 25% on dropship orders  
**Why consider:** Creates a passive DTC-like sales channel where retailers sell Floki without inventory risk. Generates brand discovery.

## Wholesale Marketplace Checklist (Before Listing)

Before applying to any marketplace, have these ready:

- [ ] High-resolution product photos (white background, lifestyle, detail shots)
- [ ] Brand logo (vector, 300+ DPI)
- [ ] Brand story (2-3 sentences: what Floki is, who it is for, why it was made)
- [ ] Product descriptions (50-150 words per product)
- [ ] Wholesale price list (see [09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md](./09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md))
- [ ] MSRP (suggested retail price) - $14-18/unit for hangers
- [ ] Product specifications (dimensions, weight, materials, colorways)
- [ ] Brand website URL: https://floki-website.vercel.app
- [ ] Business email: shopflokibrands@gmail.com
- [ ] Return/damage policy (see [09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md](./09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md))

## Boutique Retailer Outreach Rules

1. Always personalize outreach with the store name and location
2. Offer consignment for new accounts (see [09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md](./09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md) for terms)
3. When asking for interest, offer to send a lookbook or physical sample - not just a pricing sheet
4. Note that African print accent pillows pair well with hangers for a cohesive lifestyle display
5. After approval on Faire, invite existing retail prospects to order via Faire (saves commission on their first order)
