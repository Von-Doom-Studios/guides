## Subject Line Testing Framework

Run A/B tests by splitting your prospect list 50/50 on subject lines. Track open rates in Apollo.io or HubSpot.

**Rules for high-performing B2B subject lines:**
- Under 50 characters (shows fully on mobile)
- Specific, not generic (mention their company or role)
- Avoid ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation, or spam words
- Question-based subjects often outperform statement subjects
- Personalization tokens ([Hotel Name], [First Name]) increase open rate by 20-30%

**Subject line testing template:**

| Variant | Subject | Open Rate | Winner? |
|---------|---------|-----------|---------|
| A | [your subject A] | Track | |
| B | [your subject B] | Track | |

Test for 7 days, minimum 20 sends per variant, then declare a winner and standardize.
