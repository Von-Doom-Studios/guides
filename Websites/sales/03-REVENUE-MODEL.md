## Unit Economics by Channel

### Cost Foundation

- COGS per unit: $2.00
- Packaging (retail/gift): ~$0.50/unit additional
- Shipping (samples): $8-12 per sample kit, absorbed as marketing cost
- Merchant fees (Shopify/Stripe): 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Faire marketplace commission: 15% on new accounts, 0% on direct repeat

## Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly fixed-ish costs (Phase 1 - lean operations):**

| Expense | Monthly Cost |
|---------|-------------|
| HubSpot Free CRM | $0 |
| Apollo.io (Basic) | $49 |
| Klaviyo (up to 500 contacts) | $0 |
| Shopify Basic | $39 |
| Make.com (Core) | $10 |
| QuickBooks Simple Start | $30 |
| Misc (domain, email, storage) | $15 |
| **Total Monthly Overhead** | **$143** |

Break-even monthly: $143 / ~$4.00 avg gross profit per unit = 36 units/month  
At 36 units/month = 432 units/year = ~$2,700 revenue at blended B2B pricing  
Floki covers overhead with a single STR order of 12 units ($78 gross profit) plus routine B2B activity.

**This is a lean-margin business at low volumes.** The real lever is volume. At 1,000 units/month, overhead is less than 1% of revenue.
