## Tier Scoring System

| Tier | Criteria | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| A | Estimated deal $5,000+, decision-maker contact known or findable, strong fit, short sales cycle | Prioritize. Personal outreach. Book calls. Send samples. |
| B | Estimated deal $1,000-$5,000, likely fit, contact findable, some sales cycle friction | Standard sequence. Follow up 3x. Offer sample. |
| C | Estimated deal under $1,000, uncertain fit, or long/uncertain sales cycle | Bulk sequence only. Move to Nurture if no response after 3 touches. |

**Deal size estimates are based on:** channel pricing, company size, property count, and likely order volume.

## Status Key

- **Not Contacted** - no outreach yet
- **Outreach Sent** - email sent, awaiting response
- **Followed Up** - Day 5 follow-up sent
- **Final Follow-Up** - Day 12 final sent
- **Warm** - responded, in conversation
- **Meeting Scheduled** - call booked
- **Proposal Sent** - pricing/line sheet sent
- **Negotiation** - terms being discussed
- **Closed Won** - deal signed
- **Closed Lost** - not moving forward
- **Nurture** - long-term, revisit in 60 days

## How to Find More Prospects Systematically

### Apollo.io (Primary Tool)

1. Go to apollo.io and create account (free or Basic at $49/month)
2. Search by Job Title + Industry + Company Size
   - "Purchasing Manager" + "Hotels and Motels" + 50-500 employees
   - "Director of Operations" + "Real Estate" + "Short-Term Rentals"
   - "FF&E Procurement" + "Hospitality Design"
   - "Buyer" + "Boutique Retail" + "Home Goods"
3. Filter by location: New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, Atlanta, Nashville
4. Export verified emails directly to HubSpot or CSV
5. Target: add 25-50 new contacts per week during Phase 1 outreach

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator

1. Use "People" search with filters:
   - Title: "Procurement Director," "Purchasing Manager," "Director of Operations"
   - Industry: "Hospitality," "Real Estate"
   - Company headcount: 50-5,000
2. Filter by "Changed jobs in last 90 days" - new decision-makers are more open to new vendors
3. Connection request + InMail (see [07-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md](./07-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md) for LinkedIn scripts)
4. Export to CSV, enrich emails with Apollo.io
