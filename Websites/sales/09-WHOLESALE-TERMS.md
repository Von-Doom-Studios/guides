## NET Payment Processing

### Shopify B2B Native (Primary Recommendation)

As of April 2026, Shopify's B2B wholesale features are available to all merchants on any paid plan (not just Plus). Features include:

- Company profiles with custom price lists per buyer
- Net-30/Net-60 payment terms on wholesale orders
- B2B portal URL (shareable link for each wholesale account)
- Draft orders for custom wholesale quotes
- Automatic order confirmation and invoice emails

**Setup:** In Shopify admin, go to Customers > Companies, create a company for each wholesale account, assign price list, set payment terms.

**Cost:** Included in all Shopify plans ($39/month Basic)

### Faire Wholesale Marketplace (Secondary)

Faire is a wholesale marketplace used by 700,000+ boutique retailers. Floki can list products and receive inbound wholesale orders.

- Faire pays out immediately, collecting from retailers on Net-60 terms
- Faire commission: 15% on new retailer accounts, 0% on returning accounts
- Automatic net terms, net payment processing, and retailer vetting handled by Faire
- Apply at faire.com/brand-application

**When to use:** Phase 2 when boutique retail channel is active. Faire generates passive inbound wholesale orders.

### Alternative: Orderchamp or Tundra

Similar to Faire but with 0% commission (Tundra). Good for testing additional wholesale marketplace channels. See [10-RETAILER-TARGETS.md](./10-RETAILER-TARGETS.md).

## Consignment Agreement Terms

For new boutique retail accounts where Net-30 payment is not yet established.

**Consignment terms:**
- Floki ships 12-24 units to the retailer on consignment
- Retailer pays only for units sold, within 60 days of delivery
- Retailer sets their own retail price (recommended: $14-18/unit for 50-80% markup)
- Unsold inventory returned at retailer's shipping cost after 90 days, or renewed for another 90-day period
- Retailer is responsible for inventory in their possession (damage clause below)

**Consignment agreement must include:**
- Product description, quantity, and colorway breakdown
- Agreed consignment period (90 days)
- Payment schedule (remit payment for sold units by the 1st of each month)
- Return condition requirements (hangers must be in original condition, no damage)
- Liability clause: retailer liable for damage or loss after delivery
- Signed by retailer owner/buyer

**When to use consignment:** New boutique retail accounts with no prior order history. Convert to Net-30 after first successful consignment cycle.

## Return and Damage Policy

### B2B Wholesale (Hotel, STR, Retail, Fashion)

- **Order errors (wrong color/quantity sent):** Floki replaces at no charge within 14 days of delivery
- **Defective units:** Floki replaces defective units if reported within 30 days of delivery with photo documentation
- **Change of mind returns:** Not accepted after order confirmation. Buyer may return unused units for store credit within 14 days if order was placed in error.
- **Damage in transit:** File claim with carrier. Floki provides documentation for insurance claims.

### DTC (Shopify)

- 30-day return window for unused products
- Buyer pays return shipping unless product is defective
- Refund processed within 5 business days of receiving return
- No exchanges - reorder at full price

### Consignment Returns

- Unsold consignment inventory returned at retailer's expense after 90-day period
- Damaged units are not accepted as returns. Retailer invoiced at $7.50/unit for damaged product.
