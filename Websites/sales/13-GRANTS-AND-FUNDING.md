# 13 - Grants and Funding: Floki Brands

Non-dilutive funding sources and grant programs Floki can apply for. Focus is on grants, competitions, and programs that do not require giving up equity. Investment capital section included for later reference but is not a priority.

**Related files:** [02-SALES-STRATEGY.md](./02-SALES-STRATEGY.md) | [03-REVENUE-MODEL.md](./03-REVENUE-MODEL.md)

## Why This Matters

Floki's current inventory is 1,000 units. At an average wholesale price of $5.50-7.50/unit, that's $5,500-7,500 in potential revenue from existing stock. Scaling to $200K-1M requires significant reorder capital. Grants provide cash without dilution.

Estimated capital needs:
- Phase 1 ($0-50K revenue): $5,000-10,000 for reorder, packaging, samples
- Phase 2 ($50K-200K revenue): $15,000-30,000 for inventory, trade shows, tools
- Phase 3 ($200K-1M revenue): $50,000-100,000+ for inventory at scale, team, warehousing

## Tier 1 - High Priority (Apply Now or Next Cycle)

### Black Ambition Prize
- **What:** Pharrell Williams' non-profit competition for Black and Hispanic entrepreneurs
- **Award:** Up to $1,000,000 (grand prize). Smaller awards of $25K-250K also given.
- **Eligibility:** Black or Hispanic founder, for-profit company, consumer products eligible
- **Cycle:** Annual. 2025 cycle closed. 2026 cycle expected to open Q4 2025 or Q1 2026 - check blackambitionprize.com
- **What's needed:** Application, pitch video, business plan, financial projections, proof of traction
- **Why Floki qualifies:** Black-owned consumer product brand with clear market differentiation, B2B traction potential, and cultural significance (African print line)
- **Action:** Subscribe for 2026 cycle alerts at blackambitionprize.com. Prepare pitch materials.
- **Priority:** VERY HIGH - largest single non-dilutive award available

### Shopify Build Black Program
- **What:** Shopify's grant and resource program for Black entrepreneurs on the platform
- **Award:** Varies. Previous rounds included $50,000+ in grants, 12 months Shopify Advanced free, mentorship
- **Eligibility:** Black-owned business using Shopify
- **Cycle:** Periodic cohorts. Check shopify.com/build-black
- **What's needed:** Shopify store active, business plan, revenue goals
- **Priority:** HIGH - Floki is already on Shopify

### Amazon Black Business Accelerator
- **What:** Amazon program providing grants, marketing credits, and resources to Black-owned businesses selling on Amazon
- **Award:** Up to $10,000 in product imaging/marketing credits, free account management, A+ content creation
- **Eligibility:** Black-owned business, must sell on Amazon (or be willing to)
- **Cycle:** Rolling applications
- **How to apply:** sell.amazon.com/programs/black-business-accelerator
- **What's needed:** Business registration, product ready to sell
- **Action:** Consider listing Floki on Amazon as a secondary DTC channel. The accelerator benefits reduce the cost of Amazon entry significantly.
- **Priority:** HIGH - rolling admission, tangible benefits

### Target Forward Founders
- **What:** Target's program for BIPOC and underrepresented consumer product founders
- **Award:** Mentorship, retail buyer introductions, potential placement in Target stores. Not a direct cash grant but access to the largest mass-market retailer.
- **Eligibility:** BIPOC-owned consumer products brand
- **Cycle:** Annual cohorts. Check target.com/forward-founders
- **What's needed:** Product samples, brand deck, sales data
- **Why it matters:** Getting into Target even as a test/limited run would be transformational for volume. This is a Phase 3 play.
- **Priority:** HIGH for long-term, apply when traction exists (Phase 2)

## Tier 2 - Strong Fit (Apply in Phase 1-2)

### NAACP Grants and Business Programs
- **What:** NAACP partners with multiple organizations to provide grants for Black-owned businesses
- **Where:** naacp.org/find-resources/grants
- **Award:** Varies by partner program ($5,000-50,000)
- **Action:** Check the current cycle and partner programs. Multiple opportunities rotate throughout the year.
- **Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH

### SBA Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA)
- **What:** Federal agency providing grants, contracts, and procurement opportunities to minority-owned businesses
- **Where:** mbda.gov
- **Award:** Business consulting, contract facilitation, and connections to federal procurement
- **Eligibility:** Minority-owned business
- **Relevant angle:** Federal hotels and government lodging facilities procure supplies through SAM.gov. MBDA can help navigate federal procurement.
- **Priority:** MEDIUM - more relevant at Phase 2-3 scale

### Camelback Ventures
- **What:** Fellowship program for underrepresented entrepreneurs
- **Award:** $40,000 non-dilutive capital + fellowship program
- **Eligibility:** Founders of color
- **Cycle:** Annual cohorts
- **Where:** camelbackventures.org
- **Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH

### FedConnect / SAM.gov - Government Procurement
- **What:** Federal procurement portal. Hotels on military bases, government facilities, and federal lodging all procure supplies through this system.
- **Where:** sam.gov (register as a vendor) and fedconnect.net
- **What's needed:** DUNS number (now UEI - Unique Entity Identifier), SAM.gov registration (free), NAICS codes for your product category
- **NAICS codes for Floki:** 423220 (Home Furnishing Merchant Wholesalers), 339999 (All Other Miscellaneous Manufacturing)
- **Why:** Government hotel and lodging contracts are massive. Getting on the approved vendor list opens a channel most competitors don't pursue.
- **Action:** Register on SAM.gov immediately (free, takes 2-4 weeks to process). This is not a grant but a procurement channel.
- **Priority:** MEDIUM - register early, pursue contracts at scale

## Tier 3 - Worth Monitoring

### New Voices Foundation
- **What:** $1 billion fund for women of color entrepreneurs (founded by Sundial Brands / Richelieu Dennis)
- **Award:** Varies. Grants, investment, and mentorship.
- **Eligibility:** Women of color founders. Note: if Floki has a female co-founder or partner, this opens up.
- **Where:** newvoicesfoundation.org
- **Priority:** LOW unless eligibility applies

### Wish Local Empowerment Grants
- **What:** $500-2,000 grants for Black-owned small businesses
- **Where:** Check wish.com for current program status
- **Priority:** LOW - small amounts but easy application

### Local and State Grants

**New York State:**
- Empire State Development (ESD): esd.ny.gov - various small business grant programs
- NYC Small Business Services: nyc.gov/sbs - grants and zero-interest loans
- MWBE (Minority and Women-Owned Business Enterprise) Certification: Apply through NYC or NYS. Opens doors to government contracts and set-aside procurement programs.
- How to get MWBE certified: esd.ny.gov/doing-business-ny/mwbe (state level) or nyc.gov/mwbe (city level)

**Action:** Apply for MWBE certification immediately. It's free, takes 60-90 days, and opens access to government procurement set-asides.

## What You Need Ready for Most Applications

Prepare these materials once and reuse across applications:

1. **Business registration documents** (LLC/Corp filing, EIN)
2. **Founder bio and headshot** (1 paragraph + professional photo)
3. **Business plan** (2-3 pages: problem, solution, market, traction, financials)
4. **Financial projections** (use [03-REVENUE-MODEL.md](./03-REVENUE-MODEL.md))
5. **Pitch deck** (8-10 slides)
6. **Product photos** (professional, white background + lifestyle context)
7. **Proof of traction** (orders, revenue, press mentions, partnerships - even early-stage)
8. **2-minute pitch video** (founder on camera explaining the brand and opportunity)

## Investment Capital (Deprioritized)

Not a current priority per Anthony. Included for reference when timing is right.

**Potential paths:**
- Angel investors: Raise $50K-250K to fund inventory and scale. Platforms: AngelList, Republic, WeFunder
- Friends and family round: $10K-50K for Phase 1 capital
- Revenue-based financing: Shopify Capital, Clearco, Pipe - borrow against future revenue, no equity dilution
- Crowdfunding: Kickstarter or Indiegogo for product launch (also serves as marketing)

**Shopify Capital** is worth noting: once Floki's Shopify store has sales history, Shopify offers automatic revenue-based loans/advances. No application required - they offer based on sales data. This could be the simplest source of growth capital in Phase 2.
