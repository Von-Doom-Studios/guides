4. Co-marketing angle: cross-promote on socials, shared audience overlap
5. Timeline: initial outreach Month 2, target first partnership signed by Month 4

## Personal Stylist Referral Program

Low-friction, high-trust channel. Stylists recommend Floki to clients during closet edits. No inventory for them to hold.

**Program structure:**
- Trade pricing: $9.00/unit (vs. $9.99 DTC retail)
- Referral commission: 15% on first client order, 20% on all subsequent orders from that client
- No MOQ, no inventory required
- Unique referral link or code tracked via Shopify discount codes
- Monthly commission payout via PayPal or direct deposit

**Target stylists (from prospect database):**
- Beckie+Martina (NYC, celebrity styling)
- Amanda Sanders (NYC, celebrity personal shopper)
- Klosets by Kelsey (closet installs - direct hanger referral)
- The New York Stylist (closet refresh service)
- @krwardrobedesign (celebrity stylist, 11.3K Instagram)

**Scaling the stylist program:**
1. Launch with 5-10 stylists in Month 2
2. Send free sample kit (6 hangers, mixed colors) to each accepted stylist
3. Create a simple landing page: flokibrands.com/stylists with program details and sign-up form
4. After 10 active stylists: create a private Instagram Close Friends group or Slack channel for product updates, early access, and community
5. Goal: 25 active stylists by Month 6, generating $500-2,000/month in referral revenue

**Finding more stylists:**
- Instagram hashtags: #personalstyle, #closetstyle, #wardroberefresh, #closetorganization
- Search Apollo.io for "personal stylist" OR "closet consultant" OR "wardrobe stylist" in NYC, LA, Chicago, Atlanta, Miami
- The Association of Image Consultants International (AICI) directory: aici.org/find-a-consultant

## Interior Designer Affiliate Program

Interior designers specify products for residential and commercial projects. One designer can influence 5-50+ purchases per year.

**Program structure:**
- Trade pricing: $7.50/unit for residential projects (same as boutique wholesale)
- Volume pricing for hospitality projects: follows FF&E tiers in [09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md](./09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md)
- 10% referral commission on any DTC orders from clients they refer
- Featured designer spotlight on Floki website and social channels
- Early access to new colorways and product lines

**Target designer segments:**
- Boutique residential designers (closet design, bedroom, walk-in)
- Hospitality interior designers (hotel room design specs)
- Model home / real estate staging designers

**How to find designers:**
- NEWH (Network of the Hospitality Industry) member directory: newh.org
- American Society of Interior Designers (ASID): asid.org/find-a-pro
- Interior Design Society (IDS): interiordesignsociety.org
- HD Expo and BDNY exhibitor and attendee lists
- Houzz Pro directory: houzz.com/professionals
- Apollo.io search: "interior designer" + "hospitality" or "residential"

**Outreach approach:**
1. Send a physical sample kit with a one-page product spec sheet
2. Follow up via email: "I sent you a sample of our woven aluminum hangers. Would love to know if they fit into any current or upcoming projects."
3. Offer to create a trade account with net-30 terms
4. For hospitality designers: reference the FF&E procurement channel and offer to spec Floki into their next hotel project

## Hotel Brand Ambassador Program

For hotel properties that become repeat buyers. Turn satisfied hotel clients into advocates who drive referrals.

**Program structure:**
- Qualification: must have placed at least 2 orders totaling 100+ units
- Benefits: 5% additional discount on all future orders (stacks with volume tiers), featured case study on Floki website, co-marketing on social media
- Obligation: allow Floki to use hotel name and photography in marketing materials
- Referral: 5% discount credit on next order for every new hotel they refer that places an order

**Target properties for ambassador program (long-term, Phase 2-3):**
- First 3-5 hotel clients who place repeat orders
- Prioritize boutique and lifestyle properties with strong social/PR presence
- Properties with multiple locations (referral to sister properties)

**Marketing value:**
- "Used by [Hotel Name]" social proof on website and outreach materials
- Guest encounter -> DTC purchase loop becomes measurable with QR code or card in wardrobe
- Photo rights for product-in-context imagery (hotel wardrobe shots)

## Wedding and Event Planner Referral Channel

Event planners outfit gift bags, VIP suites, and wardrobe areas. Seasonal but high-margin.

**Opportunity:**
- Bridal suite closet staging with Floki hangers (bride + wedding party garments)
- Groomsmen gift sets (4-pack or 8-pack mixed colors)
- VIP event wardrobe areas (fashion shows, brand activations, photo shoots)
- Wedding welcome bags (paired with other premium items)

**Pricing:** Corporate gifting rates apply ($13-15/unit for gift sets, MOQ 12)

**How to find planners:**
- The Knot vendor directory: theknot.com
- WeddingWire vendor directory: weddingwire.com
- Zola vendor directory: zola.com
- Instagram: #weddingplanner, #eventplanner + major cities
- Apollo.io: "wedding planner" OR "event planner" in NYC, LA, Miami, Atlanta, Chicago

**Outreach approach:**
1. Position as a premium upgrade to the standard event garment handling
2. Lead with the visual: "Imagine your bride's gown on a luxury woven hanger instead of a wire one"
3. Offer a sample kit for their next event
4. Seasonal timing: outreach in January-February (peak wedding planning) and August-September (fall wedding season)

## Real Estate Staging Companies

Staging companies furnish homes for sale. Closets are part of the staging - premium hangers make empty closets look intentional.

**Opportunity:**
- Premium hangers in staged closets create the "lived-in luxury" look buyers respond to
- Repeat purchases: staging companies rotate inventory across 5-20+ properties
- Low MOQ works here: 12-24 hangers per staged property

**Pricing:** STR Management rates ($6.50/unit, MOQ 12) or boutique retail ($7.50/unit, MOQ 24) depending on volume

**Target companies:**
- Meridith Baer Home (luxury staging, LA/NYC/SF) - meridithbaer.com
- Staged to Sell (NYC metro) - stagedtoselldesign.com
- Showhomes (national franchise) - showhomes.com
- White Orchid Interiors (luxury staging) - whiteorchidinteriors.com
- Local staging companies in each target market (NYC, LA, Miami, Atlanta, Chicago)

**How to find more:**
- Real Estate Staging Association (RESA): realestatestagingassociation.com/find-a-stager
- Houzz Pro directory filtered by "home staging"
- Apollo.io: "home staging" OR "real estate staging" in target cities
- Ask boutique retail partners and interior designers for referrals

**Outreach approach:**
1. "Your staged closets are missing one detail" - visual-first pitch
2. Offer 12-unit starter kit at STR pricing
3. Before/after photos of staged closets with Floki hangers (create this content in Month 2-3)
4. Propose a recurring supply agreement: auto-ship replacement hangers quarterly
