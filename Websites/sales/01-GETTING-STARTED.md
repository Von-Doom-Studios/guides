## Ground Rules

**1. B2B first, always.**  
DTC is the brand anchor but not the primary revenue engine in Phase 1 or Phase 2. FF&E procurement, hotel direct, and STR management have the highest revenue-per-unit leverage. Lead with hospitality.

**2. No outreach without a functioning website.**  
The website at https://floki-website.vercel.app must be presentable before any cold outreach. Shopify cart (Phase 2) is not required to start B2B outreach, but the site needs to convey brand credibility.

**3. All outreach from one email.**  
shopflokibrands@gmail.com is the single sender address. Do not mix personal or agency emails. Track all outreach in HubSpot CRM.

**4. No em dashes in any external communications.**  
Use hyphens or rewrite the sentence. This applies to emails, line sheets, proposals, and any customer-facing document.

**5. Escalation rule.**  
Orders under $2,000: VD-Growth handles. Orders $2,000+: escalate to Anthony for negotiation sign-off.

**6. Update the pipeline after every touchpoint.**  
If an email goes out, update [06-PIPELINE.md](./06-PIPELINE.md). If a prospect responds, update. If a deal closes, update. Real-time data beats end-of-week recaps.

**7. Automate everything you can.**  
See [04-AUTOMATION-STACK.md](./04-AUTOMATION-STACK.md) for the full tool stack. The goal is for follow-up sequences, CRM updates, and reporting to run without manual intervention.

## Process Summary: From Cold to Closed

1. **Source prospects** - Use [05-PROSPECTS.md](./05-PROSPECTS.md) as starting database. Enrich with Apollo.io and LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
2. **Score leads** - A/B/C tier based on deal size, likelihood, and strategic value. Prioritize A-tier.
3. **Send initial outreach** - Use templates from [08-EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md](./08-EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md). Personalize [First Name] and [Company].
4. **Run follow-up sequence** - Day 5 and Day 12 follow-ups (automated via Apollo.io or Saleshandy).
5. **Respond to warm leads within 24 hours** - Send pricing from [09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md](./09-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md). Offer sample kit.
6. **Book 15-minute call for A-tier leads** (FF&E, hotel groups, multi-property STR).
7. **Send proposal / line sheet** - Include product specs, pricing, color options, sample availability.
8. **Negotiate and close** - Escalate deals above $2,000 to Anthony.
9. **Fulfill and invoice** - Process via Shopify B2B or Faire. Record in QuickBooks.
10. **Nurture won accounts** - Automated 60-day reorder reminder via Klaviyo or HubSpot.
