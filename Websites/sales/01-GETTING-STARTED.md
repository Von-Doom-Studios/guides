## The Process in Brief

1. **Read the strategy** — start with `02-SALES-STRATEGY.md` to understand channel priorities and goals
2. **Find prospects** — `03-PROSPECTS.md` has all 52 targets sorted by channel
3. **Track the pipeline** — update `04-PIPELINE.md` as deals move through stages
4. **Send outreach** — follow `05-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md` for sequencing, use templates from `06-EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md`
5. **Report weekly** — copy the template from `reports/` and fill in actuals every Friday
