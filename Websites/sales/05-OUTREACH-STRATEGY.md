## Channel Priority

B2B first. DTC is the brand anchor, not the primary revenue driver at this stage.

| Priority | Channel | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1 | FF&E Procurement Firms | Highest leverage — one firm = 100-500+ units across multiple properties |
| 2 | Hotel Direct Wholesale | Large orders, repeat buyers |
| 3 | STR Management Companies | Growing market, premium positioning |
| 4 | Boutique Retail | Steady reorders, brand visibility |
| 5 | Fashion Brands | Cultural alignment + volume potential |
| 6 | Personal Stylists | Referral engine, strong margins, no MOQ |
| 7 | Corporate Gifting | Seasonal, high margin, platform leverage |
| 8 | DTC (Floki Website) | Requires Dev + Marketing — brand anchor, not primary |

## Outreach Sequencing

Each prospect moves through this sequence:

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | Initial outreach email (channel-specific template) |
| Day 5 | Follow-up email if no response |
| Day 12 | Final follow-up email |
| Day 12+ | Move to Nurture — revisit in 60 days if still no response |

If they respond at any point: update pipeline status, respond within 24 hours, route to next step (call, sample, proposal).

## Sample / Line Sheet Process

When a prospect expresses interest:
1. Confirm their channel (retail, hotel, stylist, etc.)
2. Send the relevant pricing from `07-WHOLESALE-TERMS.md`
3. Offer to send a sample kit if they want to see the product
4. Book a 15-minute intro call if they're high-value (FF&E, hotel groups)
