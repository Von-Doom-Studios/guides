## Channel Priority (B2B First)

Volume moves through B2B at this stage. DTC is brand anchor, not primary revenue driver.

1. **FF&E Procurement Firms** - highest leverage (one firm = 100-500+ units)
2. **Hotel Direct Wholesale** - large orders, repeat buyers
3. **STR Management Companies** - growing market, premium positioning
4. **Boutique Retail** - steady reorders, brand visibility
5. **Fashion Brands** - brand alignment + volume
6. **Personal Stylists** - referral engine, strong margins
7. **Corporate Gifting** - seasonal, high margin
8. **DTC (Floki Website)** - requires Dev + Marketing to land
