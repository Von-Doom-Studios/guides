## Channel Priority

### Why B2B First

One hotel deal at 500 units = $1,875 revenue at the 500+ volume tier. One STR management company with 100 properties at 12 units each = 1,200 units = $7,800. One FF&E procurement firm managing multiple hotel openings = potentially $15,000+ in a single project.

DTC requires marketing spend, ad creative, SEO, and customer acquisition infrastructure. B2B requires a phone and a well-crafted email. At zero revenue, the highest-leverage path is direct relationship selling to buyers who buy in volume.

### Channel Priority Order

| Priority | Channel | Rationale |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | FF&E Procurement Firms | Highest leverage. One firm = multiple properties = 500-2,000+ units per year. |
| 2 | Hotel Direct Wholesale | Large recurring orders. New openings are time-sensitive buyers. |
| 3 | STR Management Companies | Growing market, lower price sensitivity, lower MOQ (12 units). |
| 4 | Corporate Gifting Platforms | High margin ($13-15/unit). Seasonal spikes, scalable. |
| 5 | Boutique Retail | Brand visibility + steady reorders + cultural credibility. |
| 6 | Fashion Brands | Cultural alignment + wholesale volume + co-brand potential. |
| 7 | Personal Stylists | Referral engine. Low overhead, no MOQ, 78% margin. |
| 8 | DTC (Floki Website) | Brand anchor and hospitality validation loop. Scales in Phase 2+. |

## Key Assumptions and Risks

**Assumptions:**
- Manufacturer can scale to 50,000+ unit orders within 6-8 weeks
- Hotel/FF&E pricing holds competitive vs. existing suppliers (Braiform, Hangers.com, etc.)
- Shopify B2B native wholesale features (available to all merchants as of April 2026) cover wholesale portal needs without Shopify Plus
- DTC conversion rate of 2-3% is achievable with proper brand positioning

**Risks:**
- Long B2B sales cycles (FF&E projects take 6-18 months from spec to purchase)
- Competitor price pressure in hotel OS&E category
- Manufacturer lead time disruption
- Domain deliverability issues if email warm-up is skipped

**Mitigations:**
- Start outreach early, build pipeline now even if purchase timelines are long
- Differentiate on aesthetics and brand story, not just price
- Maintain reorder buffer: trigger new PO at 200 units remaining
- Warm up email domain before any mass outreach (see [07-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md](./07-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md))
