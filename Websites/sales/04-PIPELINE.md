## Pipeline Stages

1. **Not Contacted** - Identified, no outreach yet
2. **Outreach Sent** - Email sent, awaiting response (Day 1)
3. **Followed Up** - Day 5 follow-up sent
4. **Final Follow-Up** - Day 12 final follow-up sent
5. **Warm** - Responded, in active conversation
6. **Meeting Scheduled** - Call booked
7. **Proposal Sent** - Pricing / line sheet sent
8. **Negotiation** - Terms being discussed
9. **Closed Won** - Deal signed
10. **Closed Lost** - Not moving forward
11. **Nurture** - Long-term, revisit later
