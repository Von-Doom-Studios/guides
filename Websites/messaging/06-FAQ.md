## Orders & Shipping

**How long does shipping take?**
Most orders ship within 1–2 business days and arrive within 3–5 business days via standard shipping. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order is on its way.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Not yet — we currently ship within the US. International shipping is coming. If you're outside the US, email us at shopflokibrands@gmail.com and we'll let you know when your region is available.

**Can I change or cancel my order?**
If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate the change. Once it's shipped, we're unable to modify it.

**How do I track my order?**
You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order ships. You can also check order status in your account if you created one at checkout.

## Returns & Refunds

**What is your return policy?**
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original condition. We don't charge a restocking fee.

**How do I start a return?**
Email us at shopflokibrands@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you instructions and a return label within 1–2 business days.

**When will I receive my refund?**
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive your return. You'll be notified by email when it's complete.

**What if my item arrived damaged?**
Email us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the damage. We'll send a replacement at no charge.

## Product Care

**How do I care for the woven hangers?**
Wipe clean with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid submerging in water or using harsh cleaners. The weave is durable but should be kept dry.

**How do I care for the African print pillows?**
The pillow cover can be removed and hand-washed in cold water. Do not machine wash. Air dry flat to preserve the fabric and pattern.

**Will the phone case protect my phone from drops?**
The sneaker cases are built with impact-resistant material and provide protection from everyday drops. They are not rated for extreme impact.

**Do the hanger colors fade over time?**
No — the dye is set into the weave and holds well with normal use and care. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight.
