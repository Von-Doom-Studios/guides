## Brand Health Signals

Beyond direct performance metrics, track these as brand health indicators:

- **Direct traffic growth.** People typing floki.com or similar directly is a signal of brand recognition.
- **Branded search volume.** Track via Google Search Console — people searching "Floki hangers" or "Floki brand" indicates awareness.
- **Save rate on Instagram.** Saves (not just likes) signal purchase consideration. A high save rate means people are coming back.
- **Referral traffic from hotel/trade contexts.** Any inbound traffic from trade publications, hotel websites, or wholesale directories is a brand health signal.

## Reporting Structure

### Weekly (internal)

- Top-performing content
- Any anomalies — traffic spike, conversion drop, unusual engagement
- Paid campaign check if running

### Monthly Report

1. Revenue summary (Shopify)
2. Email performance
3. Social growth and engagement
4. Paid performance (if active)
5. Top 3 content pieces and why they worked
6. What worked / what didn't
7. Priorities for next month

### Campaign Post-Mortem

After every campaign:
- Did we hit the KPI?
- What creative performed best?
- What would we change?
- Update the campaign entry in [06-CAMPAIGNS.md](./06-CAMPAIGNS.md)
