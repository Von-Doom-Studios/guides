### 1. Floki Website Launch

**Brand:** Floki
**Type:** Brand awareness / Product launch
**Status:** Planning
**Timeline:** TBD — pending website readiness

#### Objective

Establish Floki's brand presence at launch. Drive initial DTC traffic and first sales. Introduce the brand to trade prospects actively sourcing.

#### Audiences

- **DTC — The Considerate Buyer:** First impression. Make the brand feel immediately credible and considered.
- **Trade — Hotel Direct / STR:** Secondary launch awareness. The website needs to be live and professional before any B2B outreach continues.

#### Key Message

*"Designed for spaces that demand more."*

The launch does not introduce a product. It introduces a standard.

#### Channels

- [ ] Instagram — organic launch post + product reels
- [ ] TikTok — product showcase content
- [ ] Email — launch email to initial list
- [ ] Paid Meta — warm audience retargeting post-launch
- [ ] Website — all pages live

### 2. Trade Activation — Hotels and FF&E

**Brand:** Floki
**Type:** B2B brand awareness
**Status:** Planning — dependent on website + product photography
**Timeline:** Concurrent with website launch

#### Objective

Give the 52 active sales prospects a brand asset that communicates Floki's positioning as clearly as the product itself. The website should do the closing work after the email creates the opening.

#### Key Message

*"Floki products are specified by boutique hotels because guests notice the details. Your guests would too."*

#### Channels

- [ ] Direct outreach (Ops-managed, see `Websites/sales/05-OUTREACH-STRATEGY.md`)
- [ ] Website — trade-specific sections functional
- [ ] Line sheet / PDF asset

#### Notes

Marketing's job here is to ensure the brand assets (website, photography, voice) support the sales team's outreach. The sales team closes. Marketing makes the brand credible enough to get the meeting. See `Websites/sales/02-SALES-STRATEGY.md` for B2B channel priorities.
