# Content Strategy — Floki Marketing

Content pillars, formats, cadence, and ownership for all three brands.

---

## Floki

### Brand Content Pillars

| Pillar | What It Is | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | The thing itself — features, craft, detail | Close-up of hanger weave, pillow pattern, case finish |
| **Lifestyle** | The life our customer lives | Minimal wardrobe inspo, styled home corners, sneaker shelf |
| **Culture** | The heritage behind the products | African textile traditions, craft stories, artisan process |
| **Social Proof** | Real people, real reactions | Customer photos, reviews, UGC |

**Ratio (starting point):** 40% Lifestyle / 30% Product / 20% Culture / 10% Social Proof

---

### Formats by Channel

#### Instagram
| Format | Pillar | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Static grid post | Product / Lifestyle | 3–4x per week |
| Reel (15–30s) | Lifestyle / Culture | 2x per week |
| Story | Product / Social Proof | Daily when active |
| Carousel | Product detail / How-to | 1x per week |

**Grid aesthetic:** Alternating editorial product and lifestyle. No back-to-back product posts. Color palette stays within brand — no warm/cool whiplash.

#### TikTok
| Format | Pillar | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Product showcase (15–30s) | Product | 2–3x per week |
| Lifestyle / vibe clip | Lifestyle | 2x per week |
| Cultural story | Culture | 1x per week |

**Tone on TikTok:** Slightly more direct than Instagram. Can lean into trends where they fit naturally — never forced.

#### Email
| Type | Purpose | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Launch email | Announce new arrivals or campaigns | Per campaign |
| Product spotlight | Single product, single focus | Bi-weekly |
| Newsletter | Brand story, culture, roundup | Monthly |
| Abandoned cart | Recover lost sales | Automated |

**Email rules:** Subject lines under 8 words. Body copy under 150 words for campaigns. One CTA per email.

---

### Content Calendar Rhythm

**Weekly:**
- Mon: Plan the week's posts
- Tue–Thu: Publish primary content
- Fri: Review performance, schedule weekend content
- Weekend: Light activity — Stories, reposts

**Monthly:**
- Review top-performing content
- Update `08-ANALYTICS.md` with results
- Plan next month's campaign and content needs
- Brief design team on new visual needs (`04-VISUAL-STRATEGY.md`)

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Identify need** — campaign brief or editorial calendar slot
2. **Write copy** — use brand voice guide (`02-BRAND-VOICE.md`)
3. **Brief visuals** — create entry in `04-VISUAL-STRATEGY.md`
4. **Design produces assets** — design team runs their workflow
5. **Review** — marketing reviews against brand and campaign intent
6. **Publish** — schedule or post directly
7. **Track** — log results in `08-ANALYTICS.md`
