## Reporting Structure

### Weekly Review (internal)
- Top-performing content this week
- Any anomalies (traffic spike, drop in conversion)
- Paid campaign check-in if running

### Monthly Report
Full snapshot across all channels:
1. Revenue summary (Shopify)
2. Email performance
3. Social growth and engagement
4. Paid performance (if active)
5. Top 3 content pieces
6. What worked / what didn't
7. Priorities for next month

### Campaign Post-Mortem
After every campaign:
- Did we hit the KPI?
- What creative performed best?
- What would we do differently?
- Update the campaign entry in `05-CAMPAIGNS.md`
