# Marketing — Getting Started

This folder is owned by **VD-Marketing**. All marketing strategy, brand guidance, and copy work for Floki, VonDoom Studios, and Floating Objects Studios lives here.

---

## Brand Thesis

**"Designed for spaces that demand more."**

This line is the foundation of all Floki marketing. It governs the website, campaigns, social, email, and every piece of copy that goes out. See `02-BRAND-VOICE.md` for how to apply it.

---

## Brand Origin Story (summary)

Floki was born from decades of travel through the world's most considered spaces — boutique hotels in Marrakech, design studios in Milan, penthouse suites in New York. The conviction behind the brand is simple: the products that fill a space either honor it or diminish it. Floki was built to do the former. See `05-EXPLORE.md` in `Websites/messaging/` for the full narrative.

---

## Dual Audience

**Individuals (DTC):** Style-conscious consumers aged 25–45. They buy once and buy right.

**Trade / B2B:** Hotels, boutique properties, FF&E buyers, short-term rental operators. The site doesn't sell B2B directly — it gives trade buyers confidence enough to reach out.

## Workflow

1. All new copy starts from the brand voice in `02-BRAND-VOICE.md`
2. Website copy is tracked in `03-WEBSITE-COPY.md`, full copy in `Websites/messaging/`
3. Visual needs are briefed in `04-VISUAL-STRATEGY.md` — design team picks them up
4. Campaign work lives in `05-CAMPAIGNS.md`
5. Dev handoff posted in #dev when pages are approved
