## Instagram

### Floki
**Handle:** TBD
**Goal:** Build brand awareness and drive traffic to the Shopify store.
**Audience:** Style-conscious consumers, 25–45, home decor + fashion crossover.
**Tone:** Editorial, minimal. Caption style matches brand voice — short, no filler.
**Grid strategy:** Alternating product and lifestyle. Consistent color temperature.

**Key tactics:**
- Reels prioritized for reach
- Product carousels for detail and saves
- Stories for real-time engagement and social proof
- Link in bio → Shopify store

### VonDoom Studios
**Goal:** Showcase work, attract agency clients.
**Tone:** Confident, professional. Work speaks loudest.

### Floating Objects Studios
**Goal:** Build reputation in animation industry. Attract production clients and talent.
**Tone:** Creative, craft-focused.

## TikTok

### Floki
**Goal:** Reach younger end of target demographic. Drive product discovery.
**Tone:** Direct, engaging. Fits naturally into lifestyle/aesthetic content trends.
**Content approach:**
- Short product showcases (15–30s)
- Lifestyle styling clips
- "Unboxing" or reveal format where appropriate
- Never cringe. If a trend doesn't fit Floki naturally, skip it.

## Pinterest

### Floki
**Goal:** Long-term SEO-driven traffic. Pinterest is a discovery engine for home decor and lifestyle.
**Approach:** Pin every product image and lifestyle image with keyword-rich descriptions.
**Boards to create:**

- Woven Hanger Inspiration

## Paid — Meta (Instagram / Facebook)

### Floki
**Status:** Pre-launch planning
**Budget:** TBD
**Priority campaigns:**
1. Launch awareness — broad interest targeting, lifestyle creative
2. Retargeting — website visitors, abandoned cart
3. Product-specific — individual product ads to interest audiences

**Creative approach:** Reuse top-performing organic creative. Always test 2 ad variants minimum. Lead with product or lifestyle visual — never text-heavy.

**KPIs:** ROAS, CPM, CTR, conversion rate. See `08-ANALYTICS.md`.
